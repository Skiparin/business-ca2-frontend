Person, Company and Documentation

Here we will explain a litte about waht options you have when you click the links

  • We have the person site. In here you have different choices.

    • First option: Get all persons - This will give you a list of all persons in the database where they are from and they hobbies.
    • Second option: Get person by id - In here you will type in a id which will show you only the user you want.
    • Thrid option: Get person by Zip - This will give you a list of all the persons with the given zip.
    • Fourth option: Create Person - This will create a user Person in the database, with the given Firstname, Lastname and E-mail Address.
    • Fifth option: Edit Person - Here you can edit a specific user by thier id.
    • Sixt option: Delete Person - here you can delete a specific user by thier id.

  • We have the Company site. In here you have different choices.

    • First option: Get all companies - This will give you a list of all companies in the database and thier addresses
    • Second option: Get Company by CVR - This will search for the given CVR and if it exists you will only get that company
    • Thrid option: Create company - Here you can create a company by entering. Company name, CVR number, Description of the company,
      Market value of the company, Numbers of Employees and an email
    • Fourth option: Edit company - Here you can edit in the data of a specific company by its CVR number
    • Fifth option: Delete company - Here you can delete a company by a specific CVR number
