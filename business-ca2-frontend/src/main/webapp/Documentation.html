    We startede by making the backend.
    we chose to make the entities and thier relations from java.

    Here you can see how our relations.

    Here you can see our facades
    We made facades to InfonEntity, Phone and address. Then we made a main facade when we call the 3 other mapper facades. When our Rest calls a metode in the main facade, the main facade then calls one of the 3 mapper facades. The mapper facade then get or makes the chance in the database and returns either a Person or a Company.
